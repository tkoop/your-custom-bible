The Triumphant Messiah
(Acts 4:23–31)

1Why do the nations ragea

and the peoples plot in vain?

2The kings of the earth take their stand

and the rulers gather together,

against the LORD

and against Hishis Anointed Oneone:b

3“Let us break Theirtheir chains

and cast away Theirtheir cords.”

4The Oneone enthroned in heaven laughs;

the Lord taunts them.

5Then Hehe rebukes them in Hishis anger,

and terrifies them in Hishis fury:

6“I have installed Mymy King on Zion,

upon Mymy holy mountain.”

7I will proclaim the decree

spoken to Meme by the LORD:

“You are Mymy Sonson;

today I have become Youryour Fatherfather.c

8Ask Meme, and I will make the nations Youryour inheritance,

the ends of the earth Youryour possession.

9You will break themd with an iron sceptresceptrescepter;

You will shatter them like pottery.e”

10Therefore be wise, O kings;

be admonished, O judges of the earth.

11Serve the LORD with fear,

and rejoice with trembling.

12Kiss the Sonson, lest Hehe be angry

and you perish in your rebellion,

when Hishis wrath ignites in an instant.

Blessed are all who take refuge in Himhim.
