The Day of Trouble

For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David.

1May the LORD answer you in the day of trouble;

may the name of the God of Jacob protect you.

2May HeheHe send you help from the sanctuary

and sustain you from Zion.

3May HeheHe remember all your gifts

and look favourablyfavourablyfavorably on your burnt offerings.

Selah

4May HeheHe give you the desires of your heart

and make all your plans succeed.

5May we shout for joy at your victory

and raise a banner in the name of our God.

May the LORD grant all your petitions.

6Now I know that the LORD saves HishisHis anointed;

He answers him from HishisHis holy Heavenheavenheaven

with the saving power of HishisHis right hand.

7Some trust in chariots and others in horses,

but we trust in the name of the LORD our God.

8They collapse and fall,

but we rise up and stand firm.

9O LORD, savea the king.

Answer us on the day we call.
