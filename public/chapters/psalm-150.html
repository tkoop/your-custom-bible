Let Everything That Has Breath Praise the LORD

1Hallelujah!a

Praise God in Hishis sanctuary.

Praise Himhim in Hishis mighty heavens.b

2Praise Himhim for Hishis mighty acts;

praise Himhim for Hishis excellent greatness.

3Praise Himhim with the sound of the horn;

praise Himhim with the harp and lyre.

4Praise Himhim with tambourine and dancing;

praise Himhim with strings and flute.

5Praise Himhim with clashing cymbals;

praise Himhim with resounding cymbals.

6Let everything that has breath praise the LORD!

Hallelujah!

 

 

Footnotes:

 

 

1 a Or Hallelu YAH, meaning Praise the LORD; also in verse 6

 

1 b Or in the expanse of his might or in the firmament of his might; see also Genesis 1:6–8.
