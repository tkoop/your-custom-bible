(Matthew 17:1–13; Luke 9:28–36; 2 Peter 1:16–21)

The Boy with an Evil Spirit
(Matthew 17:14–18; Luke 9:37–42)

14When they returned to the other disciples, they saw a large crowd around them, and scribes arguing with them. 15As soon as all the people saw Jesus, they were filled with awe and ran to greet HimhimHim.
