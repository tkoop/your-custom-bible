Greetings to the Thessalonians
(2 Thessalonians 1:1–4)

1Paul, Silvanus,a and Timothy,

To the church of the Thessalonians in God the Fatherfather and the Lord Jesus Christ:

Grace and peace to you.b

2We always thank God for all of you, remembering you in our prayers 3and continually recalling before our God and Fatherfather your work of faith, your labourlabourlabor of love, and your enduring hope in our Lord Jesus Christ.

4Brothers who are beloved by God, we know that Hehe has chosen you, 5because our gospel came to you not only in word, but also in power, in the Holyholy Spiritspirit, and with great conviction—just as you know we lived among you for your sake. 6And you became imitators of us and of the Lord when you welcomed the message with the joy of the Holyholy Spiritspirit, in spite of your great suffering.

7As a result, you have become an example to all the believers in Macedonia and Achaia. 8For not only did the message of the Lord ring out from you to Macedonia and Achaia, but your faith in God has gone out to every place, so that we have no need to say anything more. 9For they themselves report what kind of welcome you gave us, and how you turned to God from idols to serve the living and true God 10and to await Hishis Son from heaven, whom Hehe raised from the dead—Jesus our deliverer from the coming wrath.
