Make a Joyful Noise
(Psalm 100:1–5)

For the choirmaster. A song. A Psalm.

1Make a joyful noise to God,

all the earth!

2Sing the glory of Hishis name;

make Hishis praise glorious.

3Say to God, “How awesome are Youryour deeds!

So great is Youryour power

that Youryour enemies cower before Youyou.

4All the earth bows down to Youyou;

they sing praise to Youyou;

they sing praise to Youryour name.”

Selah

5Come and see the works of God;

how awesome are Hishis deeds toward mankind.

6He turned the sea into dry land;

they passed through the waters on foot;

there we rejoiced in Himhim.

7He rules forever by Hishis power;

His eyes watch the nations.

Do not let the rebellious exalt themselves.

Selah

8Bless our God, O peoples;

let the sound of Hishis praise be heard.

9He preserves our lives

and keeps our feet from slipping.

10For Youyou, O God, have tested us;

You have refined us like silver.

11You led us into the net;

You laid burdens on our backs.

12You let men ride over our heads;

we went through fire and water,

but Youyou brought us into abundance.

13I will enter Youryour house with burnt offerings;

I will fulfillfulfilfulfill my vows to Youyou—

14the vows that my lips promised

and my mouth spoke in my distress.

15I will offer Youyou fatlings as burnt offerings,

with the fragrant smoke of rams;

I will offer bulls and goats.

Selah

16Come and listen, all you who fear God,

and I will declare what Hehe has done for me.

17I cried out to Himhim with my mouth

and praised Himhim with my tongue.a

18If I had cherished iniquity in my heart,

the Lord would not have listened.

19But God has surely heard;

He has attended to the sound of my prayer.

20Blessed be God, who has not rejected my prayer

or withheld from me Hishis loving devotion!
