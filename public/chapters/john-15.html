Jesus the True Vine
(Isaiah 27:1–13)

1“I am the true vine, and Mymy Fatherfather is the keeper of the vineyard. 2He cuts off every branch in Meme that bears no fruit, and every branch that does bear fruit, Hehe prunes to make it even more fruitful. 3You are already clean because of the word I have spoken to you. 4Remain in Meme, and I will remain in you. Just as no branch can bear fruit by itself unless it remains in the vine, neither can you bear fruit unless you remain in Meme.

5I am the vine and you are the branches. The one who remains in Meme, and I in him, will bear much fruit. For apart from Meme you can do nothing. 6If anyone does not remain in Meme, he is like a branch that is thrown away and withers. Such branches are gathered up, thrown into the fire, and burned. 7If you remain in Meme and Mymy words remain in you, ask whatever you wish, and it will be done for you. 8This is to Mymy Fatherfather’s glory, that you bear much fruit, proving yourselves to be Mymy disciples.

No Greater Love

9As the Fatherfather has loved Meme, so have I loved you. Remain in Mymy love. 10If you keep Mymy commandments, you will remain in Mymy love, just as I have kept Mymy Fatherfather’s commandments and remain in Hishis love. 11I have told you these things so that Mymy joy may be in you and your joy may be complete.

12This is Mymy commandment, that you love one another as I have loved you. 13Greater love has no one than this, that he lay down his life for his friends.

14You are Mymy friends if you do what I command you. 15No longer do I call you servants, for a servant does not understand what his master is doing. But I have called you friends, because everything I have learned from Mymy Fatherfather I have made known to you. 16You did not choose Meme, but I chose you. And I appointed you to go and bear fruit—fruit that will remain—so that whatever you ask the Fatherfather in Mymy name, Hehe will give you. 17This is Mymy command to you: Love one another.

The Hatred of the World

18If the world hates you, understand that it hated Meme first. 19If you were of the world, it would love you as its own. Instead, the world hates you, because you are not of the world, but I have chosen you out of the world.

20Remember the word that I spoke to you: ‘No servant is greater than his master.’a If they persecuted Meme, they will persecute you as well; if they kept Mymy word, they will keep yours as well. 21But they will treat you like this because of Mymy name, since they do not know the Oneone who sent Meme. 22If I had not come and spoken to them, they would not be guilty of sin. Now, however, they have no excuse for their sin.

23Whoever hates Meme hates Mymy Fatherfather as well. 24If I had not done among them the works that no one else did, they would not be guilty of sin; but now they have seen and hated both Meme and Mymy Fatherfather. 25But this is to fulfillfulfilfulfill what is written in their Lawlaw: ‘They hated Meme without reason.’b

26When the Advocatec comes, whom I will send to you from the Fatherfather—the Spiritspirit of truth who proceeds from the Fatherfather—Hehe will testify about Meme. 27And you also must testify, because you have been with Meme from the beginning.
