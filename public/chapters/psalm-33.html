Praise to the Creator
(Psalm 148:1–14)

1Rejoice in the LORD, O righteous ones;

it is fitting for the upright to praise HimhimHim.

2Praise the LORD with the harp;

make music to HimhimHim with ten strings.

3Sing to HimhimHim a new song;

play skillfully with a shout of joy.

4For the word of the LORD is upright,

and all HishisHis work is trustworthy.

5The LORD loves righteousness and justice;

the earth is full of HishisHis loving devotion.

6By the word of the LORD the heavens were made,

and all the stars by the breath of HishisHis mouth.

7He piles up the waters of the sea;

He puts the depths into storehouses.

8Let all the earth fear the LORD;

let all the people of the world revere HimhimHim.

9For HeheHe spoke, and it came to be;

He commanded, and it stood firm.

10The LORD frustrates the plans of the nations;

He thwarts the devices of the peoples.

11The counsel of the LORD stands forever,

the purposes of HishisHis heart to all generations.

12Blessed is the nation whose God is the LORD,

the people HeheHe has chosen as HishisHis inheritance!

13The LORD looks down from Heavenheavenheaven;

He sees all the sons of men.

14From HishisHis dwelling place HeheHe gazes

on all who inhabit the earth.

15He shapes the hearts of each;

He considers all their works.

16No king is saved by his vast army;

no warrior is delivered by his great strength.

17A horse is a vain hope for salvation;

even its great strength cannot save.

18Surely the eyes of the LORD are on those who fear HimhimHim,

on those whose hope is in HishisHis loving devotion

19to deliver them from death

and keep them alive in famine.

20Our soul waits for the LORD;

He is our help and our shield.

21For our hearts rejoice in HimhimHim,

since we trust in HishisHis holy name.

22May YouryourYour loving devotion rest on us, O LORD,

as we put our hope in YouyouYou.
