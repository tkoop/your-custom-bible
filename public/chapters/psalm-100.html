Make a Joyful Noise
(Psalm 66:1–20)

A Psalm of thanksgiving.

1Make a joyful noise to the LORD,

all the earth.

2Serve the LORD with gladness;

come into HishisHis presence with joyful songs.

3Know that the LORD is God.

It is HeheHe who made us, and we are HishisHis;a

we are HishisHis people, and the sheep of HishisHis pasture.

4Enter HishisHis gates with thanksgiving

and HishisHis courts with praise;

give thanks to HimhimHim and bless HishisHis name.

5For the LORD is good,

and HishisHis loving devotion endures forever;

His faithfulness continues to all generations.

3 a Or and not we ourselves
