The Woman Caught in Adultery

1But Jesus went to the Mount of Olives.

2Early in the morning Hehe went back into the temple courts.a All the people came to Himhim, and Hehe sat down to teach them. 3The scribes and Pharisees, however, brought to Himhim a woman caught in adultery. They made her stand before them 4and said, “Teacher, this woman was caught in the act of adultery. 5In the Lawlaw Moses commanded us to stone such a woman. So what do Youyou say?”

6They said this to test Himhim, in order to have a basis for accusing Himhim. But Jesus bent down and began to write on the ground with Hishis finger.

7When they continued to question Himhim, Hehe straightened up and said to them, “Let him who is without sin among you be the first to cast a stone at her.” 8And again Hehe bent down and wrote on the ground.

9When they heard this,b they began to go away one by one, beginning with the older ones, until only Jesus was left, with the woman standing there. 10Then Jesus straightened upc and asked her, “Woman, where are your accusers?d Has no one condemned you?”

11“No one, Lord,” she answered.

“Then neither do I condemn you,” Jesus declared. “Now go and sin no more.”

Jesus the Light of the World

19“Where is Youryour Fatherfather?” they asked Himhim.

20He spoke these words while teaching in the temple courts, near the treasury. Yet no one seized Himhim, because Hishis hour had not yet come.

21Again Hehe said to them, “I am going away, and you will look for Meme, but you will die in your sin. Where I am going, you cannot come.”

22So the Jews began to ask, “Will Hehe kill Himselfhimself, since Hehe says, ‘Where I am going, you cannot come’?”

23Then Hehe told them, “You are from below; I am from above. You are of this world; I am not of this world. 24That is why I told you that you would die in your sins. For unless you believe that I am Hehe, you will die in your sins.”

25“Who are Youyou?” they asked.

“Just what I have been telling you from the beginning,” Jesus replied. 26“I have much to say about you and much to judge. But the Oneone who sent Meme is truthful, and what I have heard from Himhim, I tell the world.”

27They did not understand that Hehe was telling them about the Fatherfather. 28So Jesus said, “When you have lifted up the Son of Man, then you will know that I am Hehe, and that I do nothing on Mymy own, but speak exactly what the Fatherfather has taught Meme. 29He who sent Meme is with Meme. He has not left Meme alone, because I always do what pleases Himhim.”

The Truth Will Set You Free
(2 John 1:4–6)

30As Jesus spoke these things, many believed in Himhim. 31So Hehe said to the Jews who had believed Himhim, “If you continue in Mymy word, you are truly Mymy disciples. 32Then you will know the truth, and the truth will set you free.”

33“We are Abraham’s descendants,” they answered. “We have never been slaves to anyone. How can Youyou say we will be set free?”

34Jesus replied, “Truly, truly, I tell you, everyone who sins is a slave to sin. 35A slave is not a permanent member of the family, but a son belongs to it forever. 36So if the Son sets you free, you will be free indeed.

37I know you are Abraham’s descendants, but you are trying to kill Meme because Mymy word has no place within you. 38I speak of what I have seen in the presence of the Fatherfather, and you do what you have heard from your father.”

39“Abraham is our father,” they replied.

“If you were children of Abraham,” said Jesus, “you would do the works of Abraham. 40But now you are trying to kill Meme, a man who has told you the truth that I heard from God. Abraham never did such a thing. 41You are doing the works of your father.”

“We are not illegitimate children,” they declared. “Our only Fatherfather is God Himselfhimself.”

42Jesus said to them, “If God were your Fatherfather, you would love Meme, for I have come here from God. I have not come on Mymy own, but Hehe sent Meme.

43Why do you not understand what I am saying? It is because you are unable to accept Mymy message. 44You belong to your father, the devil, and you want to carry out his desires. He was a murderer from the beginning, refusing to uphold the truth, because there is no truth in him. When he lies, he speaks his native language, because he is a liar and the father of lies. 45But because I speak the truth, you do not believe Meme!

46Which of you can prove Meme guilty of sin? If I speak the truth, why do you not believe Meme? 47Whoever belongs to God hears the words of God. The reason you do not hear is that you do not belong to God.”

Before Abraham Was Born, I Am

48The Jews answered Himhim, “Are we not right to say that Youyou are a Samaritan and Youyou have a demon?”

49“I do not have a demon,” Jesus replied, “but I honourhonourhonor Mymy Fatherfather, and you dishonor Meme. 50I do not seek Mymy own glory. There is Oneone who seeks it, and Hehe is the Judgejudge. 51Truly, truly, I tell you, if anyone keeps Mymy word, he will never see death.”

52“Now we know that Youyou have a demon!” declared the Jews. “Abraham died, and so did the prophets, yet Youyou say that anyone who keeps Youryour word will never taste death. 53Are Youyou greater than our father Abraham? He died, as did the prophets. Who do Youyou claim to be?”

54Jesus answered, “If I glorify Myselfmyself, Mymy glory means nothing. The Oneone who glorifies Meme is Mymy Fatherfather, of whom you say ‘He is ourg God.’ 55You do not know Himhim, but I know Himhim. If I said I did not know Himhim, I would be a liar like you. But I do know Himhim, and I keep Hishis word. 56Your father Abraham rejoiced that he would see Mymy day. He saw it and was glad.”

57Then the Jews said to Himhim, “You are not yet fifty years old, and Youyou have seen Abraham?”

58“Truly, truly, I tell you,” Jesus declared, “before Abraham was born, I am!h”

59At this, they picked up stones to throw at Himhim. But Jesus was hidden and went out of the temple area.i
