To You I Lift Up My Soul

Of David.a

1To YouyouYou, O LORD, I lift up my soul;

2in YouyouYou, my God, I trust.

Do not let me be put to shame;

do not let my enemies exult over me.

3Surely none who wait for YouyouYou will be put to shame;

but those who are faithless without cause will be disgraced.

4Show me YouryourYour ways, O LORD;

teach me YouryourYour paths.

5Guide me in YouryourYour truth and teach me,

for YouyouYou are the God of my salvation;

all day long I wait for YouyouYou.

6Remember, O LORD, YouryourYour compassion and loving devotion,

for they are from age to age.

7Remember not the sins of my youth,

nor my rebellious acts;

remember me according to YouryourYour loving devotion,

because of YouryourYour goodness, O LORD.

8Good and upright is the LORD;

therefore HeheHe shows sinners the way.

9He guides the humble in what is right

and teaches them HishisHis way.

10All the LORD’s ways are loving and faithful

11For the sake of YouryourYour name, O LORD,

12Who is the man who fears the LORD?

He will instruct him in the path chosen for him.

13His soul will dwell in prosperity,

and his descendants will inherit the land.

14The LORD confides in those who fear HimhimHim,

and reveals HishisHis covenant to them.

15My eyes are always on the LORD,

16Turn to me and be gracious,

17The troubles of my heart increase;

18Consider my affliction and trouble,

and take away all my sins.

19Consider my enemies, for they are many,

and they hate me with vicious hatred.

20Guard my soul and deliver me;

let me not be put to shame,

for I take refuge in YouyouYou.

21May integrity and uprightness preserve me,

22Redeem Israel, O God,
