Majestic Is His Work

1Hallelujah!a

I will give thanks to the LORD with all my heart

in the council of the upright and in the assembly.

2Great are the works of the LORD;

they are pondered by all who delight in them.

3Splendid and majestic is HishisHis work;

His righteousness endures forever.

4He has caused HishisHis wonders to be remembered;

the LORD is gracious and compassionate.

5He provides food for those who fear HimhimHim;

He remembers HishisHis covenant forever.

6He has shown HishisHis people the power of HishisHis works

by giving them the inheritance of the nations.

7The works of HishisHis hands are truth and justice;

all HishisHis precepts are trustworthy.

8They are upheld forever and ever,

enacted in truth and uprightness.

9He has sent redemption to HishisHis people;

He has ordained HishisHis covenant forever;

holy and awesome is HishisHis name.

10The fear of the LORD is the beginning of wisdom;

all who follow HishisHis precepts gain rich understanding.

His praise endures forever!

 

 

Footnotes:

 

 

1 a Or Hallelu YAH, meaning Praise the LORD. This psalm is an acrostic poem, each line beginning with the successive letters of the Hebrew alphabet.
