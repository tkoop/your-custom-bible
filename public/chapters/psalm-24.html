The Earth Is the LORD’s

A Psalm of David.

1The earth is the LORD’s, and the fullness thereof,a

the world and all who dwell therein.

2For Hehe has founded it upon the seas

and established it upon the waters.

3Who may ascend the hill of the LORD?

Who may stand in Hishis holy place?

4He who has clean hands and a pure heart,

who does not lift up his soul to an idolb

or swear deceitfully.

5He will receive blessing from the LORD

and vindication from the God of his salvation.

6Such is the generation of those who seek Himhim,

who seek Youryour face, O God of Jacob.c

Selah

7Lift up your heads, O gates!

Be lifted up, O ancient doors,

that the King of Gloryglory may enter!

8Who is this King of Gloryglory?

The LORD strong and mighty,

the LORD mighty in battle.

9Lift up your heads, O gates!

Be lifted up, O ancient doors,

that the King of Gloryglory may enter!

10Who is Hehe, this King of Gloryglory?

The LORD of Hosts—

He is the King of Gloryglory.

Selah
