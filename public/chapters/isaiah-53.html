The Suffering Servant
(Acts 8:26–40; 1 Peter 2:21–25)

1Who has believed our message?

And to whom has the arm of the LORD been revealed?a

2He grew up before Himhim like a tender shoot,

and like a root out of dry ground.

He had no stately form or majesty to attract us,

no beauty that we should desire Himhim.

3He was despised and rejected by men,

a man of sorrows, acquainted with grief.

Like one from whom men hide their faces,

He was despised, and we esteemed Himhim not.

4Surely Hehe took on our infirmities

and carried our sorrows;b

yet we considered Himhim stricken by God,

struck down and afflicted.

5But Hehe was pierced for our transgressions,

He was crushed for our iniquities;

the punishment that brought us peace was upon Himhim,

and by Hishis stripes we are healed.c

6We all like sheep have gone astray,d

each one has turned to his own way;

and the LORD has laid upon Himhim

the iniquity of us all.

7He was oppressed and afflicted,

yet Hehe did not open Hishis mouth.

He was led like a lamb to the slaughter,

and as a sheep before her shearers is silent,

so Hehe did not open Hishis mouth.

8By oppression and judgmentjudgementjudgment Hehe was taken away,

and who can recount Hishis descendants?

For Hehe was cut off from the land of the living;e

He was stricken for the transgression of Mymy people.

A Grave Assigned
(Matthew 27:57–61; Mark 15:42–47; Luke 23:50–56; John 19:38–42)

9He was assigned a grave with the wicked,

and with a rich man in Hishis death,

although Hehe had done no violence,

nor was any deceit in Hishis mouth.f

10Yet it was the LORD’s will to crush Himhim

and to cause Himhim to suffer;

and when Hishis soul is made a guilt offering,g

He will see Hishis offspring, Hehe will prolong Hishis days,

and the good pleasure of the LORD will prosper in Hishis hand.

11After the anguish of Hishis soul,

He will see the light of lifeh and be satisfied.

By Hishis knowledge Mymy righteous Servantservant will justify many,

and Hehe will bear their iniquities.

12Therefore I will allot Himhim a portion with the great,i

and Hehe will divide the spoils with the strong,j

because Hehe has poured out Hishis life unto death,

and Hehe was numbered with the transgressors.k

Yet Hehe bore the sin of many

and made intercession for the transgressors.
