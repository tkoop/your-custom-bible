Praise Awaits God in Zion

For the choirmaster. A Psalm of David. A song.

1Praise awaits Youyou, O God, in Zion;

to Youyou our vows will be fulfilled.

2O Youyou who listen to prayer,

all people will come to Youyou.

3When iniquities prevail against me,

You atone for our transgressions.

4Blessed is the one Youyou choose

and bring near to dwell in Youryour courts!

We are filled with the goodness of Youryour house,

the holiness of Youryour temple.

5With awesome deeds of righteousness Youyou answer us,

O God of our salvation,

the hope of all the ends of the earth

and of the farthest seas.

6You formed the mountains by Youryour power,

having girded Yourselfyourself with might.

7You stilled the roaring of the seas,

the pounding of their waves,

and the tumult of the nations.

8Those who live far away fear Youryour wonders;

You make the dawn and sunset shout for joy.a

9You attend to the earth and water it;b

with abundance Youyou enrich it.

The streams of God are full of water,

for Youyou prepare our grain

by providing for the earth.c

10You soak its furrows and level its ridges;

You soften it with showers and bless its growth.

11You crown the year with Youryour bounty,

and Youryour paths overflow with plenty.

12The pastures of the wilderness overflow;

the hills are robed with joy.

13The pastures are clothed with flocks,

and the valleys are decked with grain.

They shout in triumph;

indeed, they sing.
