For the choirmaster. A Psalm of the sons of Korah.

that Youryour people may rejoice in Youyou?

7Show us Youryour loving devotion, O LORD,

8I will listen to what God the LORD will say;

for Hehe will surely speak peace to Hishis people and Hishis saints;

He will not let them return to folly.

9Surely Hishis salvation is near to those who fear Himhim,

that Hishis glory may dwell in our land.

10Loving devotion and faithfulness have joined together;

12The LORD will indeed provide what is good,

and our land will yield its increase.

13Righteousness will go before Himhim
