(Matthew 12:9–14; Luke 6:6–11)

3Then Jesus said to the man with the withered hand, “Stand up among us.” 4And HeheHe asked them, “Which is lawful on the Sabbath: to do good or to do evil, to save life or to destroy it?”

5Jesus looked around at them with anger and sorrow at their hardness of heart. Then HeheHe said to the man, “Stretch out your hand.” So he stretched it out, and it was restored.
