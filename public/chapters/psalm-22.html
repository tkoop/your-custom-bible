The Psalm of the Cross
(Matthew 27:32–56; Mark 15:21–41; Luke 23:26–43; John 19:16–30)

For the choirmaster. To the tune of “The Doe of the Dawndawn.” A Psalm of David.

1My God, my God,

why have Youyou forsaken me?a

Why are Youyou so far from saving me,

so far from my words of groaning?

2I cry out by day, O my God,

but Youyou do not answer,

and by night,

but I have no rest.

3Yet Youyou are holy,

enthroned on the praises of Israel.

4In Youyou our fathers trusted;

they trusted and Youyou delivered them.

5They cried out to Youyou and were set free;

they trusted in Youyou and were not disappointed.

6But I am a worm and not a man,

scorned by men and despised by the people.

7All who see me mock me;

they sneer and shake their heads:

8“He trusts in the LORD,

let the LORD deliver him;

let the LORD rescue him,

since Hehe delights in him.”b

9Yet Youyou brought me forth from the womb;

You made me secure at my mother’s breast.

10From birth I was cast upon Youyou;

from my mother’s womb Youyou have been my God.

11Be not far from me,

for trouble is near

and there is no one to help.

12Many bulls surround me;

strong bulls of Bashan encircle me.

13They open their jaws against me

like lions that roar and maul.

14I am poured out like water,

and all my bones are disjointed.

My heart is like wax;

it melts away within me.

15My strengthc is dried up like a potsherd,

and my tongue sticks to the roof of my mouth.

You lay me in the dust of death.

16For dogs surround me;

a band of evil men encircles me;

they have pierced my hands and feet.d

17I can count all my bones;

they stare and gloat over me.

18They divide my garments among them

and cast lots for my clothing.e

19But Youyou, O LORD, be not far off;

O my Strengthstrength, come quickly to help me.

20Deliver my soul from the sword,

my precious life from the power of wild dogs.

21Save me from the mouth of the lion;

at the horns of the wild oxen Youyou have answered me!

22I will proclaim Youryour name to my brothers;

I will praise Youyou in the assembly.f

23You who fear the LORD, praise Himhim!

All descendants of Jacob, honourhonourhonor Himhim!

All offspring of Israel, revere Himhim!

24For Hehe has not despised or detested

the torment of the afflicted.

He has not hidden Hishis face from him,

but has attended to his cry for help.

25My praise for Youyou resounds in the great assembly;

I will fulfillfulfilfulfill my vows before those who fear Youyou.

26The poor will eat and be satisfied;

those who seek the LORD will praise Himhim.

May your hearts live forever!

27All the ends of the earth

will remember and turn to the LORD.

All the families of the nations

will bow down before Himhim.

28For dominion belongs to the LORD

and Hehe rules over the nations.

29All the rich of the earth will feast and worship;

all who go down to the dust will kneel before Himhim—

even those unable to preserve their lives.

30Posterity will serve Himhim;

they will declare the Lord to a new generation.

31They will come and proclaim Hishis righteousness

to a people yet unborn—

all that Hehe has done.
