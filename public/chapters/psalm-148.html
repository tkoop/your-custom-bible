Praise the LORD from the Heavens
(Psalm 33:1–22)

1Hallelujah!a

Praise the LORD from the heavens;

praise Himhim in the highest places.b

2Praise Himhim, all Hishis angels;

praise Himhim, all Hishis heavenly hosts.

3Praise Himhim, O sun and moon;

praise Himhim, all you shining stars.

4Praise Himhim, O highest heavens,

and you waters above the skies.

5Let them praise the name of the LORD,

for Hehe gave the command and they were created.

6He established them forever and ever;

He issued a decree that will never pass away.

7Praise the LORD from the earth,

all great sea creatures and ocean depths,

8lightning and hail, snow and clouds,

powerful wind fulfilling Hishis word,

9mountains and all hills,

fruit trees and all cedars,

10wild animals and all cattle,

crawling creatures and flying birds,

11kings of the earth and all peoples,

princes and all rulers of the earth,

12young men and maidens,

old and young together.

13Let them praise the name of the LORD,

for Hishis name alone is exalted;

His splendoursplendoursplendor is above the earth and the heavens.

14He has raised up a horn for Hishis people,

the praise of all Hishis saints,

of Israel, a people near to Himhim.

Hallelujah!

 

 

Footnotes:

 

 

1 a Or Hallelu YAH, meaning Praise the LORD; also in verse 14

 

1 b See Matthew 21:9, Mark 11:10, and Luke 19:38.
