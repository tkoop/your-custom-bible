Be My Rock of Refuge

1In Youyou, O LORD, I have taken refuge;

let me never be put to shame.

2In Youryour justice, rescue and deliver me;

incline Youryour ear and save me.

3Be my rock of refuge,

where I can always go.

Give the command to save me,

for Youyou are my rock and my fortress.

4Deliver me, O my God, from the hand of the wicked,

from the grasp of the unjust and ruthless.

5For Youyou are my hope, O Lord GOD,

my confidence from my youth.

6I have leaned on Youyou since birth;

You pulled me from my mother’s womb.

My praise is always for Youyou.

7I have become a portent to many,

but Youyou are my strong refuge.

8My mouth is filled with Youryour praise

and with Youryour splendoursplendoursplendor all day long.

9Do not discard me in my old age;

do not forsake me when my strength fails.

10For my enemies speak against me,

and those who lie in wait for my life conspire,

11saying, “God has forsaken him;

pursue him and seize him,

for there is no one to rescue him.”

12Be not far from me, O God.

Hurry, O my God, to help me.

13May the accusers of my soul

be ashamed and consumed;

may those who seek my harm

be covered with scorn and disgrace.

14But I will always hope

and will praise Youyou more and more.

15My mouth will declare Youryour righteousness

and Youryour salvation all day long,

though I cannot know their full measure.

16I will enter in the strength of the Lord GOD;

I will proclaim Youryour righteousness—Yoursyours alone.

17O God, Youyou have taught me from my youth,

and to this day I proclaim Youryour marvellousmarvellousmarvelous deeds.

18Even when I am old and greygreygray,

do not forsake me, O God,

until I proclaim Youryour power to the next generation,

Your might to all who are to come.

19Your righteousness reaches to the heavens, O God,

You who have done great things.

Who, O God, is like Youyou?
