Deliver Me, O LORD!
(2 Samuel 15:13–29)

A Psalm of David, when he fled from his son Absalom.

1O LORD, how my foes have increased!

How many rise up against me!

2Many say of me,

“God will not deliver him.”

Selaha

3But Youyou, O LORD, are a shield around me,

my glory, and the Oneone who lifts my head.

4To the LORD I cry aloud,

and Hehe answers me from Hishis holy mountain.

Selah

5I lie down and sleep;

I wake again, for the LORD sustains me.

6I will not fear the myriads

set against me on every side.

7Arise, O LORD!

Save me, O my God!

Strike all my enemies on the jaw;

break the teeth of the wicked.

8Salvation belongs to the LORD;

may Youryour blessing be on Youryour people.

Selah

 

 

Footnotes:

 

 

2 a Selah or Interlude is probably a musical or literary term; here and throughout the Psalms.
