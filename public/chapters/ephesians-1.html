Paul’s Greeting to the Ephesians
(Acts 19:8–12; Revelation 2:1–7)

1Paul, an apostle of Christ Jesus by the will of God,

To the saints in Ephesus,a the faithful in Christ Jesus:

2Grace and peace to you from God our FatherfatherFather and the Lord Jesus Christ.

Spiritual Blessings
(Romans 8:28–34)

3Blessed be the God and FatherfatherFather of our Lord Jesus Christ, who has blessed us in Christ with every spiritual blessing in the heavenly realms. 4For HeheHe chose us in HimhimHim before the foundation of the world to be holy and blameless in HishisHis presence. In love 5HeheHe predestined us for adoption as HishisHis sons through Jesus Christ, according to the good pleasure of HishisHis will, 6to the praise of HishisHis glorious grace, which HeheHe has freely given us in the BelovedbelovedBeloved OneoneOne.

7In HimhimHim we have redemption through HishisHis blood, the forgiveness of our trespasses, according to the riches of HishisHis grace 8that HeheHe lavished on us with all wisdom and understanding. 9And HeheHe has made known to us the mystery of HishisHis will according to HishisHis good pleasure, which HeheHe purposed in Christ 10as a plan for the fullness of time, to bring all things in Heavenheavenheaven and on Earthearthearth together in Christ.

11In HimhimHim we were also chosen as God’s own, having been predestined according to the plan of HimhimHim who works out everything by the counsel of HishisHis will, 12in order that we, who were the first to hope in Christ, would be for the praise of HishisHis glory.

13And in HimhimHim, having heard and believed the word of truth—the gospel of your salvation—you were sealed with the promised HolyholyHoly SpiritspiritSpirit, 14who is the pledge of our inheritance until the redemption of those who are God’s possession, to the praise of HishisHis glory.

Spiritual Wisdom
(1 Corinthians 2:6–16)

15For this reason, ever since I heard about your faith in the Lord Jesus and your love for all the saints, 16I have not stopped giving thanks for you, remembering you in my prayers, 17that the God of our Lord Jesus Christ, the glorious FatherfatherFather, may give you a spirit of wisdom and revelation in your knowledge of HimhimHim.

18I ask that the eyes of your heartb may be enlightened, so that you may know the hope of HishisHis calling, the riches of HishisHis glorious inheritance in the saints, 19and the surpassing greatness of HishisHis power to us who believe. These are in accordance with the working of HishisHis mighty strength, 20which HeheHe exerted in Christ when HeheHe raised HimhimHim from the dead and seated HimhimHim at HishisHis right hand in the heavenly realms, 21far above all rule and authority, power and dominion, and every name that is named, not only in the present age but also in the one to come.

22And God put everything under HishisHis feet and made HimhimHim head over everything for the church, 23which is HishisHis body, the fullness of HimhimHim who fills all in all.
