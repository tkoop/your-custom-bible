You Turned My Mourning into Dancing

A Psalm. A song for the dedication of the temple. Of David.

1I will exalt Youyou, O LORD,

for Youyou have lifted me up

and have not allowed my foes

to rejoice over me.

2O LORD my God, I cried to Youyou for help,

and Youyou healed me.

3O LORD, Youyou pulled me up from Sheol;

You spared me from descending into the Pit.

4Sing to the LORD, O you Hishis saints,

and praise Hishis holy name.a

5For Hishis anger is fleeting,

but Hishis favourfavourfavor lasts a lifetime.

Weeping may stay the night,

but joy comes in the morning.

6In prosperity I said,

“I will never be shaken.”

7O LORD, Youyou favored me;

You made my mountain stand strong.

When Youyou hid Youryour face,

I was dismayed.

8To Youyou, O LORD, I called,

and I begged my Lord for mercy:

9“What gain is there in my bloodshed,b

in my descent to the Pit?c

Will the dust praise Youyou?

Will it proclaim Youryour faithfulness?

10Hear me, O LORD, and have mercy;

O LORD, be my helper.”

11You turned my mourning into dancing;

You peeled off my sackcloth and clothed me with joy,

12that my heart may sing Youryour praises and not be silent.

O LORD my God, I will give thanks forever.

 

 

Footnotes:

 

 

4 a Or and praise the memorial of His holiness; see Exodus 3:15.

 

9 b Or in my destruction

 

9 c Or to corruption
