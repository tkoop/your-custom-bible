You Have Heard My Vows

For the choirmaster. With stringed instruments. Of David.

1Hear my cry, O God;

attend to my prayer.

2From the ends of the earth I call out to Youyou

whenever my heart is faint.

Lead me to the rock

that is higher than I.

3For Youyou have been my refuge,

a tower of strength against the enemy.

4Let me dwell in Youryour tent forever

and take refuge in the shelter of Youryour wings.

Selah

5For Youyou have heard my vows, O God;

You have given me the inheritance

reserved for those who fear Youryour name.

6Increase the days of the king’s life;

may his years span many generations.

7May he sit enthroned in God’s presence forever;

appoint Youryour loving devotion

and Youryour faithfulness to guard him.

8Then I will ever sing praise to Youryour name

and fulfillfulfilfulfill my vows day by day.
