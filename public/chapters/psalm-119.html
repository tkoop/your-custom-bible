4You have ordained Youryour precepts,

5Oh, that my ways were committed

6Then I would not be ashamed

7I will praise Youyou with an upright heart

BETH

do not let me stray from Youryour commandments.

11I have hidden Youryour word in my heart

that I might not sin against Youyou.

12Blessed are Youyou, O LORD;

teach me Youryour statutes.

13With my lips I proclaim

all the judgmentsjudgementsjudgments of Youryour mouth.

14I rejoice in the way of Youryour testimonies

15I will meditate on Youryour precepts

16I will delight in Youryour statutes;

I will not forget Youryour word.

GIMEL

17Deal bountifully with Youryour servant,

that I may live and keep Youryour word.

18Open my eyes that I may see

wondrous things from Youryour law.

19I am a stranger on the earth;

do not hide Youryour commandments from me.

20My soul is consumed with longing

for Youryour judgmentsjudgementsjudgments at all times.

21You rebuke the arrogant—

the cursed who stray from Youryour commandments.

22Remove my scorn and contempt,

for I have kept Youryour testimonies.

23Though rulers sit and slander me,

Your servant meditates on Youryour statutes.

24Your testimonies are indeed my delight;

they are my counselors.

DALETH

25My soul cleaves to the dust;

revive me according to Youryour word.

26I recounted my ways, and Youyou answered me;

teach me Youryour statutes.

27Make clear to me the way of Youryour precepts;

then I will meditate on Youryour wonders.

28My soul melts with sorrow;

strengthen me according to Youryour word.

29Remove me from the path of deceit

and graciously grant me Youryour law.

30I have chosen the way of truth;

I have set Youryour ordinances before me.

31I cling to Youryour testimonies, O LORD;

let me not be put to shame.

32I run in the path of Youryour commandments,

for Youyou will enlarge my heart.

HE

33Teach me, O LORD, the way of Youryour statutes,

and I will keep them to the end.b

34Give me understanding that I may obey Youryour law,

and follow it with all my heart.

35Direct me in the path of Youryour commandments,

for there I find delight.

36Turn my heart to Youryour testimonies

and not to covetous gain.

37Turn my eyes away from worthless things;

revive me with Youryour word.c

38Establish Youryour word to Youryour servant,

for Youryour judgmentsjudgementsjudgments are good.

44I will always obey Youryour law,

forever and ever.

47I delight in Youryour commandments

48I lift up my hands to Youryour commandments, which I love,

and I meditate on Youryour statutes.

ZAYIN

49Remember Youryour word to Youryour servant,

52I remember Youryour judgmentsjudgementsjudgments of old, O LORD,

53Rage has taken hold of me

54Your statutes are songs to me

in the house of my pilgrimage.

55In the night, O LORD, I remember Youryour name,

that I may keep Youryour law.

56This is my practice,

for I obey Youryour precepts.

HETH

57The LORD is my portion;

I have promised to keep Youryour words.

58I have sought Youryour face with all my heart;

be gracious to me according to Youryour promise.

59I considered my ways

and turned my steps to Youryour testimonies.

60I hurried without hesitating

62At midnight I rise to give Youyou thanks

69Though the arrogant have smeared me with lies,

I keep Youryour precepts with all my heart.

70Their hearts are hardd and callous,

71It was good for me to be afflicted,

72The law from Youryour mouth is more precious to me

75I know, O LORD, that Youryour judgmentsjudgementsjudgments are righteous,

79May those who fear Youyou turn to me,

those who know Youryour testimonies.

80May my heart be blameless in Youryour statutes,

that I may not be put to shame.
