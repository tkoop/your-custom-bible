I Will Exalt You, My God and King

A Psalm of praise. Of David.a

1I will exalt YouyouYou, my God and King;

I will bless YouryourYour name forever and ever.

2Every day I will bless YouyouYou,

and I will praise YouryourYour name forever and ever.

3Great is the LORD and greatly to be praised;

His greatness is unsearchable.

4One generation will commend YouryourYour works to the next,

and will proclaim YouryourYour mighty acts—

5the glorious splendoursplendoursplendor of YouryourYour majesty.

And I will meditate on YouryourYour wondrous works.

6They will proclaim the power of YouryourYour awesome deeds,

and I will declare YouryourYour greatness.

7They will extol the fame of YouryourYour abundant goodness

and sing joyfully of YouryourYour righteousness.

8The LORD is gracious and compassionate,

slow to anger and abounding in loving devotion.

9The LORD is good to all;

His compassion rests on all HeheHe has made.

10All YouyouYou have made will give YouyouYou thanks, O LORD,

and YouryourYour saints will bless YouyouYou.

11They will tell of the glory of YouryourYour kingdom

and speak of YouryourYour might,

12to make known to men YouryourYour mighty acts

and the glorious splendoursplendoursplendor of YouryourYour kingdom.

13Your kingdom is an everlasting kingdom,

and YouryourYour dominion endures through all generations.

The LORD is faithful in all HishisHis words

and kind in all HishisHis actions.b

14The LORD upholds all who fall

and lifts up all who are bowed down.

15The eyes of all look to YouyouYou,

and YouyouYou give them their food in season.

16You open YouryourYour hand

and satisfy the desire of every living thing.

17The LORD is righteous in all HishisHis ways

and kind in all HishisHis deeds.

18The LORD is near to all who call on HimhimHim,

to all who call out to HimhimHim in truth.

19He fulfills the desires of those who fear HimhimHim;

He hears their cry and saves them.

20The LORD preserves all who love HimhimHim,

but all the wicked HeheHe will destroy.

21My mouth will declare the praise of the LORD;

let every creature bless HishisHis holy name

forever and ever.
