Out of the Depths

A song of ascents.

1Out of the depths

I cry to Youyou, O LORD!

2O Lord, hear my voice;

let Youryour ears be attentive to my plea for mercy.

3If Youyou, O LORD, kept track of iniquities,

then who, O Lord, could stand?

4But with Youyou there is forgiveness,

so that Youyou may be feared.

5I wait for the LORD; my soul does wait,

and in Hishis word I put my hope.

6My soul waits for the Lord

more than watchmen wait for the morning—

more than watchmen wait for the morning.

7O Israel, put your hope in the LORD,

for with the LORD is loving devotion,

and with Himhim is redemption in abundance.

8And Hehe will redeem Israel

from all iniquity.
