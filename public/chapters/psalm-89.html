I Will Sing of His Love Forever

A Maskila of Ethan the Ezrahite.

with my mouth I will proclaim YouryourYour faithfulness to all generations.

13Mighty is YouryourYour arm; strong is YouryourYour hand.

14Righteousness and justice are the foundation of YouryourYour throne;

15Blessed are those who know the joyful sound,

16They rejoice in YouryourYour name all day long,

17For YouyouYou are the glory of their strength,

19You once spoke in a vision;

to YouryourYour godly ones YouyouYou said,

“I have bestowed help on a warrior;

with MymyMy sacred oil I have anointed him.

22No enemy will exact tribute;

24My faithfulness and loving devotion will be with him,

Selah

38Now, however, YouyouYou have spurned and rejected him;

You are enraged by YouryourYour anointed one.

39You have renounced the covenant with YouryourYour servant

and sullied his crown in the dust.

40You have broken down all his walls;

You have reduced his strongholds to rubble.

41All who pass by plunder him;

he has become a reproach to his neighbors.

42You have exalted the right hand of his foes;

You have made all his enemies rejoice.

43You have bent the edge of his sword

and have not sustained him in battle.

44You have ended his splendoursplendoursplendor

and cast his throne to the ground.

45You have cut short the days of his youth;

You have covered him with shame.

46How long, O LORD?

47Remember the briefness of my lifespan!

For what futility YouyouYou have created all men!

48What man can live and never see death?

Can he deliver his soul from the power of Sheol?

Selah

49Where, O Lord, is YouryourYour loving devotion of old,

which YouyouYou faithfully swore to David?

50Remember, O Lord, the reproach of YouryourYour servants,

which I bear in my heart from so many people—

51how YouryourYour enemies have taunted, O LORD,

52Blessed be the LORD forever!

Amen and amen.
