Bless the LORD, All You Servants

A song of ascents.

1Come, bless the LORD,

all you servants of the LORD

who serve by night

in the house of the LORD!

2Lift up your hands to the sanctuary

and bless the LORD!

3May the LORD, the Maker of heaven and earth,

bless you from Zion.
